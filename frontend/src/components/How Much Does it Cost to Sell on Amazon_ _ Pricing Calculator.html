PRICING

Let's talk numbers

The cost to sell in Amazon stores depends on which selling plan you choose, which category of products you sell, and how you plan to fulfill orders. The options are flexible, so you can find the combo that works best for you and your goals.
SELLING PLAN

Do you plan to sell lots of units?

Amazon offers two selling plans: Individual and Professional. The Individual plan costs $0.99 per unit sale. The Professional plan costs $39.99 per month no matter how many units you sell. If you consistently sell more than 40 units a month, the Professional plan is the best choice.
See plan comparison
REFERRAL FEES

What products will you sell?

In addition to the cost of your selling plan, Amazon charges a referral fee for each item sold. The referral fee for a lot of product categories is between 8% and 15% (with a minimum referral fee of $0.30 per sale), but check the fee schedule for specific details.
See referral fees by category
FULFILLMENT COSTS

How will you ship orders?

You can ship your own orders, or you can use Fulfillment by Amazon (FBA) and let us handle shipping, customer service, and returns. You can also combine these methods or explore some of our other options based on what works best for your business.
See fulfillment fees
Some sellers may incur additional fees, or pay for optional programs like advertising or account management.
See other costs

Getting paid and paying fees

When you set up a seller account, you’ll provide credit card and bank account information. Amazon will charge the credit card to cover your fees, and proceeds from any sales will be dispersed into your bank account every two weeks.

When you fulfill orders yourself, Amazon charges shipping rates based on the product category and shipping service selected by the customer, and then passes the amount on to you in the form of a shipping credit.
Sellers with the Professional plan set their own shipping rates (except for books, music, video, and DVD products, see credit table below). When a customer buys your product, you receive a credit from Amazon that matches the shipping rate. Keep in mind that the referral fee is calculated from the total sale price, including the item price, shipping cost, and any gift-wrapping charges.
Sellers with the Individual plan use Amazon’s set shipping rates for all products. Individual sellers have to ship orders at the set rates even if the shipping credit is less than total shipping costs, so it’s important to price items in a way that makes sure you'll earn a profit.

Other costs

As you think about the full cost of selling in Amazon stores, be sure to consider additional selling expenses you may incur, and other optional programs designed to help you increase sales.

Additional selling fees

Rental book service fees
If you sell textbook rentals, expect to pay a rental book service fee of $5.00 for each rental.
High-volume listing fees
When you have a high volume of active, non-media listings that have not sold in 12 months, we charge a monthly fee of $0.005 per eligible listing to cover cataloging costs. We waive this fee for the first 100,000 listings.
Learn more about high-volume listing fees
Refund administration fee
If you refund a customer for an order for which you have already received payment, Amazon will refund you the amount of the referral fee you paid for the item(s), minus the applicable refund administration fee, which is the lesser of $5.00 or 20% of the referral fee.

Optional programs

Fulfillment by Amazon
Amazon takes care of your shipping, returns, and customer service. Specialty FBA programs like FBA Small and Light, Subscribe & Save for FBA, FBA Dangerous Goods, Multi-Channel Fulfillment, and FBA Export help you remain competitive and pay only for the services you use.
Learn more about FBA programs and fees
Advertising
Attract customers from the time before they even realize they need your product to the time they’re buying it. Amazon Advertising use a cost-per-click, auction-based pricing model, which means you set the maximum amount you’re willing to pay when a shopper clicks your ad.
Learn more about advertising
Paid account services
Paid account management and other services are available to help craft your business plan, offer personalized coaching and training, and provide information to help you grow your sales on Amazon.
Learn about paid services
